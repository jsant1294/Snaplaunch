Common Questions

Here's what you need to know before getting started.

How fast can I launch?

With our Template System, 1-2 days. Template + Setup (Hybrid), 5-7 days. Done For You, 10-14 days. We prioritize speed without sacrificing conversion.

Do you offer ongoing support?

Yes. All packages include launch support. Done For You clients get 30 days of optimization support included. We offer optional monthly optimization packages for clients who want continued iteration.

What if I already have a website?

We can audit your current site and build a high-converting landing page or funnel alongside it. Many clients use a separate SnapLaunch system to drive leads while keeping their main site as-is.

Can you help with my messaging/offer?

Absolutely. The Discovery Call is where we dig into your offer and positioning. Done For You clients get positioning support included. We help refine your message to resonate with your ideal clients.

What happens after launch?

We monitor your conversion data and recommend optimizations. Most clients see their best results in weeks 3-6 as we refine CTAs, messaging, and targeting based on real visitor behavior.

Who actually builds the site?

SnapLaunch's team. We're not resellers—we build every project in-house. You work directly with our strategy and build team from start to finish.
